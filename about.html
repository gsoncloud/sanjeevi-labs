WHOLESALERS OF ESSENTIAL OILS AND MANUFACTURERS OF PURE OILS

It been used medicinally throughout history they are highly concentrated non-water soluble phytochemicals that are distilled from different parts of plants. They can be distilled from leaves, roots, flowers and other parts of plants and are very potent because of their concentration of phytochemicals. Essential oils have gained incredible popularity in recent years, but they are really an age-old remedy that have become popular again (though it is important to note that many modern essential oils are more concentrated than the oils used historically because of better distillation techniques).
M/S INDIAN AROMA EXPORTS started to roll out essential oil manufacturer with latest distillation techniques under Late Mr.Ram Niwas who went to IIT Madras to study Chemical engineer.He worked around the globe to learn new distillation techniques and worked on Chromatography machines to analyse the Essential oil content to its purity.The company established its manufacturing units near kannauj district in Uttar Pradesh in 1969 for distillation of 100% Pure Unblended Natural Essential oil , Organic unadulterated Herbal products and other natural material(Attars). Our products are exported across the globe and we have firm presence in Europe, Asia, North America, Middle East etc. and continuing to expand through out world by serving products of International standards.

Indian Aroma Exports is an ISO 9001: 2015 certified company ,GMP accreted company,HALAL,KOSHER,HAPCC certified. We have an ultra modern lab with Gas cartography and all our products comes with proper lab reports like Certificate of Analysis, MSDS, Gas Chromatography on customer demand.

Although we hold stocks of some key ingredients in the INDIA, most products are shipped direct from source to customer. Through efficient supply chain management, we are able to ensure fair prices for producers whilst at the same time remaining competitive.

SOURCING
Our raw materials come directly from the source. We have global contacts and have development personal relationships with wild-crafters and cultivators from unspoiled regions of the earth.

STORAGE
Fresh raw materials are stored on site in large, well maintained storage warehouses. This environment is kept scrupulously clean without the use of pesticides.

QUALITY CHECK SYSTEM
Indian Aroma Exports has been awarded the ISO 9001 certificate for quality, what this means is that we have established and apply a quality management system in our factory to design, develop, manufacture and supply Natural Extracts conforming to ISO 9001 standards. Our quality management system is audited every 6 months by an external auditor.

PRODUCTION
Our production facilities are GMP compliant and use food grade stainless steel (SS316). We use high quality valves & special welded joints to prevent leaching of heavy metals into the extracts.

We have designed the facility with maximum flexibility, allowing the production of large bulk orders or small batches at short notice. Currently, the plant is capable of handling over 15,000 kg of raw material per day.

Shipping Options
Our shipping partners are DHL, Fedex, Bluedart, First Flight, Dtdc. We also ship the goods by air as well as by sea as per buyer's requirement.

MISSION & OBJECTIVE
Indian Aroma Exports principal objective is to develop partnerships with producers and collectors of organic herbal ingredients and sell their products into the international food, cosmetics and medicines markets.

We achieve this by quality control, efficient management of the supply chain, a keen understanding of the buyer's requirements and providing an exemplary service.

Operating to the highest ethical and environmental standards, we actively support organic farming, the managed collection of wild plants, biodiversity and rural livelihoods.
